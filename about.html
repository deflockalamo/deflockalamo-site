About

Who we are

This site is run by Alamogordo area residents who think residents deserve clear, sourced information about the Flock Safety ALPR deployment in our community. We are not affiliated with any political party, candidate, or campaign. We are not affiliated with the City of Alamogordo, the Alamogordo Police Department, Holloman Air Force Base, deflock.org or Flock Safety.

What this site is — and isn't

What we are

  • An information hub. Primary documents, public records, journalism, and explainers — in one place.
  • Both-sides. We link to the case for ALPRs and the case against them, and we try to be honest about what's actually known.
  • Privacy-respecting. No analytics, no cookies, no tracking, no fingerprinting. See privacy.

What we are not

  • Not an attack on the police. We link to APD's public statements. We respect the difficulty of the job.
  • Not a campaign against a person. No personal attacks on the Police Chief, the City Manager, or any Commissioner.
  • Not a vendor of a competing product. We don't sell anything.

How the site is run

The site is a static site hosted on Cloudflare Pages. The source is plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no build step, no database, no server-side code. There is no comment section, no login, and no user accounts.

Documents uploaded to the Documents page are public records or publicly available journalism. We do not publish private information about individuals (names of camera owners, personal addresses, etc.).
