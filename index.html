Before you enter

This site is a work in progress. We're publishing it now because the issue is timely, but please read the four notes below before relying on anything you read here.

1. Still under construction

The site is missing a lot of information. Many pages are placeholders. Expect broken links, sparse sections, and rough edges. We are publishing incrementally as documents and reports come in, and as we have time to write them up.

2. Built with substantial AI assistance

The site's structure, code, and large parts of the prose were drafted with the help of AI tooling. The human maintainer curated the source material, made the editorial decisions, and is responsible for what is published — but the writing was not done by hand, page by page.

3. Lightly proof-read

The text has had at most a single pass of human review for fact-checking and tone. It has not been edited line by line. Expect:

  • Awkward phrasing, repetition, or generic AI tells
  • Possible factual errors that a careful read would catch
  • Missing nuance on a complicated topic

If something here matters to you, please verify it against the linked source documents. The links are the canonical record — the prose is a guided tour.

4. Independent, not affiliated

This site is community-run. It is not affiliated with any local government, police department, Flock Safety, or any advocacy organization. The contact information on the About page reaches one person who lives in the area and is not speaking for any institution.
